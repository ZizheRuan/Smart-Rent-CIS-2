How to start your rental journey:

Smart Searching

Type your university, preferred suburb or postcode in the search bar above to start a basic fuzzy search.

Or select your university and distance range on the left side to start a precise search. All fields are optional.

Smart Sorting

Searching by uni? Properties nearest the uni would show first. Results come with distance and duration to your uni.

Doesn't care about distance? Results would be prioritised by price and bedrooms.

Smart Aggregation

Based on the locations of universities, we've gathered the rental information from Realestate and Domain for you.

Enjoy selecting and comparing without the hassle of repeat searching on different websites, we got all the rental information you need.

Smart Rating and Review

Fear of hostile agents? Worry about your bond? Angry with slow responses from agents?
See the ratings and comments of the agents to avoid them!

Fear of hidden defects? Worry about facility condition and transportation?
We offer the ratings and comments of the property!

Story of Smart Rent

Smart Rent is a review aggregator platform created by students and for students. We have suffered from what you are suffering, we know what you need.

We are working on more universities and rental websites. Sign up with your uni email account today to begin your journey. Together, we can make the market transparent and fair!
